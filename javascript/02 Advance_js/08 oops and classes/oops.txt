Q1. does js really have classes or its an sytactic sugar over its existing prototyp
based inheritance mechanism?
Answer: Yes, JavaScript has classes, but it's a syntactic sugar over its 
existing prototype-based inheritance mechanism.

Q2. what is object oriented programming and what are its four pillars?
Answer: Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) is a programming paradigm that
uses objects and their interactions to model real-world entities and
problems. It emphasizes the idea of encapsulation, inheritance, polymorphism,
and abstraction. The four pillars of OOP are:
Encapsulation, Inheritance, Polymorphism, and Abstraction
